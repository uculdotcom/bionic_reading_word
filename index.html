(Per-Word) Bionic Converter



Bionic reading is a reading method brought to you by Renato Casutt, a typographic designer from Switzerland. The idea is to bold certain letters from a read. The method is especially helpful for people with reading and attention difficulties. Please check it out. Currently, the app is free.

With this method, I hardly skip lines when reading. However, personally, randomly bolded letters make me a bit dizzy. I instead prefer to have randomly bolded words. That's why I designed this page. Try this out and see which reading method suits you better.

Try out the bold probability slider. Also, you can click on the "Convert" button as many times as you want.
